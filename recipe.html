Cookies and Cream Cupcakes

Ingredients:

These delicious treats are guaranteed crowd-pleasers.

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups cold decaf coffee or water
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 tbsp cider vinegar

Icing:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups icing sugar
  • 1/4 cup whipping cream or milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 choc sandwich cookies such as Oreo, finely chopped

Garnish:

  • 6 chocolate sandwich cookies, quartered

How to bake these yummers:

In large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.

Whisk in coffee, oil, and vanilla.

Stir in vinegar.

Pour into 24 paper-lined muffin cups.

Bake in 350degree F oven until cake tester inserted in centre comes out clean, 18 to 20 minutes.

Icing:

In large bowl, beat butter, icing sugar, cream and vanilla until fluffy.

Fold in chopped sandwich cookies and spread over cupcakes.

Garnish:

Garnish each cupcake with cookie quarter.
